Welcome to the Wild Woolly Women

A friendly social knit-working group

We’ve been meeting since April 2009 to share our love of all things woolly. We’re not just knitters — many of us spin, weave and felt too. Whether you’re a complete beginner or a seasoned maker, you’re warmly invited to join us for fun, friendship and (of course) homemade cake.

  • 1st Wednesday (10.00–1.00pm) at Elkstone Village Hall
  • 2nd Wednesday (10.00–1.00pm) at Jolly Nice
  • 3rd Wednesday (10.00–1.00pm) at Stroud Brewery
  • 4th Wednesday (10.00–1.00pm) at Organic Farm Shop
