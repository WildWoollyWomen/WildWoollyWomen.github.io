Welcome to our Social Knit-working Group

The Wild Woolly Women have been meeting since April 2009 to share their enthusiasm for all things woolly. We are not just a knitting group! Many of us are also keen spinners and proficient weavers and felters. You are invited to join us, have some fun, learn a new skill or brush up on some old ones – all levels are welcome.

  • We meet the 1st Wednesday (10.00–1.00pm) to knit and spin, chat and eat lots of homemade cake at Elkstone Village Hall
  • 2nd Wednesday (10.00–1.00pm) at Jolly Nice.
  • 3rd Wednesday (10.00–1.00pm) at Stroud Brewery.
  • 4th Wednesday (10.00–1.00pm) at Organic Farm Shop.
Contact us: info@wildwoollywomen.co.uk
